New to WA?

You have 30 days to title & register your vehicle after it arrives here.

You may title register your vehicle by visiting our office.

Please bring the following documents:

1. The out-of-state title

and

2. Proof of Washington Emission Test

For vehicles registered in another state and owned
for less than 90 days, you must bring:

Vehicle/Vessel Bill of Sale showing the price of
the vehicle

or

Purchase agreement from the dealership with
proof of any taxes paid.
